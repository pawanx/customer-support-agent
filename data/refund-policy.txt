Refund Policy

1. Refunds are allowed within 30 days of delivery.

2. The product must have been delivered.

3. Refunds cannot be processed if a refund has already been issued.

4. Digital products are not refundable.

5. Products marked as damaged by the customer are eligible if requested within 30 days.

6. Premium customers may receive one exception with manager approval.

7. Every refund decision must include a reason.
